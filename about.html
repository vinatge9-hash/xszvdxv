Our Story

niyantri started with a single belief: coffee should be direct, intentional, and unafraid. We roast to highlight origin and process, then serve in a space designed for clarity and conversation.

What we stand for

  • • Transparency — about beans, roast, and technique.
  • • Craft — deliberate brewing and a refined menu.
  • • Community — a welcoming space for everyone.

The Roast Room

We source thoughtfully and roast to reveal the personality of each lot — not to mask it. Expect clarity in the cup and an emphasis on balance.
